Common questions

FAQ

How much does it cost
Every item has a flat, upfront price — an accent chair is $89, a queen bed frame $119, a TV mount up to 55" is $119. You always see your exact total before you book, and a small service-call fee ($35–$65 by area) covers your pro’s travel. Browse the full pricing page for every item.
When do I pay
At checkout, Stripe securely verifies your card and shows the full total before you confirm. You may see a temporary “pending” hold on your account; the actual charge is processed after the job is complete. We never see or store your card number.
How quickly can you come
Same-day and next-day availability depends on the service, location, and pro availability. After you submit a booking, we follow up quickly to confirm the appointment and pro assignment.
What’s your cancellation policy
Plans change — that’s OK. Cancel or reschedule free up to 24 hours before your appointment. Within 24 hours a small fee applies (10%, or 15% once a pro is on the way) to cover the time your pro set aside — on the service price only, never tax, and never the full amount. Just give us a heads-up rather than no-showing, and we’ll always work with you.
Do I need to provide tools or parts
Nope — your pro brings all the tools. Just have the item, its hardware and parts, and any instructions handy so they can get right to work. Lost the manual? No problem — most pros know these builds by heart.
What areas do you serve
We currently serve the Austin metro and are expanding to new areas. Enter your ZIP in the availability checker above to see if we are booking your area yet — if not, you can get on the list and we will reach out the moment we launch near you.
What if something gets damaged
We treat your home and belongings with care. In the unlikely event of damage, contact us within 48 hours and we will review it and work with you to make it right.
Can you assemble any brand
Yes — IKEA, Wayfair, Amazon, Ashley, Target, Costco, Walmart and more. If it came in a box, we can build it.
Are your pros verified
Yes — every pro completes identity verification and is personally reviewed by our team before their first job. We only work with people we’d trust in our own home.
