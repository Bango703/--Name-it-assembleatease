# AssembleAtEase Content Engine Plan

Purpose: turn each local guide into bookings, social content, Google Business Profile posts, and apartment/property outreach without cluttering the website.

## Operating Order

1. Publish or update one Austin guide on the website.
2. Link the guide to the matching service page and booking path.
3. Link the matching service page back to the guide.
4. Create the social kit from the guide.
5. Post manually first: Facebook, Google Business Profile, Instagram, LinkedIn.
6. Reuse the same guide as a short video script.
7. Reuse the same guide as an apartment/property outreach talking point.
8. Track clicks, bookings, phone calls, and quote requests from that guide.
9. Update weak guides every 60 to 90 days with better pricing, local examples, and FAQs.
10. Only automate posting after the real social profiles, permissions, and API keys are approved.

## Required Links

Every article should include:

- Service page link: `/furniture-assembly-austin-tx`, `/tv-mounting-austin-tx`, `/smart-home-installation-austin-tx`, `/business`, or `/book`.
- Booking link: `/book` or the matching `/book?service=...` path.
- Social share links: Facebook and LinkedIn.
- One follow link: current Facebook profile until Instagram and LinkedIn company profiles are live.

Every service page should include:

- Three related guides.
- One "All guides" link.
- No discontinued service links.

## Social Kit Template

Use this every time a guide is published.

Website post:

- Title: local Austin problem plus service intent.
- CTA: book the matching service.
- Internal links: matching service page, pricing, and one related guide.

Facebook post:

- Hook: "Austin move-in tip:" or "Before you mount that TV:"
- 2 to 4 short lines from the article.
- CTA: "Read the guide or book a visit."
- Link: article URL.

Instagram caption:

- Hook in first line.
- 3 practical bullets.
- CTA: "Save this before your next setup project."
- Link-in-bio target: article URL or booking page.

LinkedIn post:

- Angle: apartments, property managers, small offices, or local operations.
- Explain the business problem: time, tenant readiness, repeat work, fewer missed appointments.
- CTA: "For Austin move-ins and office setups, see the guide."

Google Business Profile post:

- Keep it direct and local.
- Mention Austin and nearby suburbs naturally.
- CTA: "Book online" or "Learn more."

Short video script:

- 0-3 sec: show the problem.
- 3-12 sec: explain the mistake or cost.
- 12-22 sec: show the better way.
- 22-30 sec: CTA to book or read the guide.

Apartment outreach talking point:

- "We help residents get moved in faster by handling furniture assembly, TV mounting, and smart home setup in one appointment."
- Mention reduced resident frustration, cleaner move-ins, and easier leasing office referrals.

## Automation Gate

Do not turn on automatic social posting until:

- Buffer API key is added as `BUFFER_API_KEY`.
- Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Google Business Profile are connected inside Buffer.
- Buffer channel IDs are added for the channels you want to publish to.
- UTM tracking is decided.
- Buffer queue/review settings are confirmed so posts do not go out at messy times.
