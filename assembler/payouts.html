How launch payouts work

Customers pay AssembleAtEase. After a job is completed and the customer payment is captured, the owner sends your payout outside this dashboard, with a current target of within 7 business days. This dashboard records payout status; it does not send funds.

This saves only your preferred method. Do not enter bank, card, Zelle, or PayPal credentials here. AssembleAtEase will separately confirm the details needed before the first payout.
