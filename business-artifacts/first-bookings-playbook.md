- Required: business verification + background check + proof of insurance. Allow ~1 week.
- Set a weekly budget cap (start ~$50/week). You only pay for actual lead contacts.
- Respond to every lead within minutes — response speed drives your LSA ranking.

> Hi [Name] — great meeting you. AssembleAtEase handles TV mounting and furniture/IKEA assembly across

asked "who assembles this?" weekly — be their referral).
