Common questions

FAQ

How much does it cost?
All prices are flat-rate — no hourly billing, no surprises. Furniture assembly starts at $69, TV mounting from $69, home repairs from $75, smart home installs from $69, and junk removal from $75. See our full pricing page for a complete breakdown.
When do I pay?
Your card is held at booking but only charged after the job is complete. We accept all major credit and debit cards through our secure Stripe-powered platform. We do not accept cash, Zelle, or Venmo.
How quickly can you come?
We offer same-day and next-day availability for most services. Once you submit a booking we confirm your appointment within 1 hour.
What’s your cancellation policy?
Cancel at least 24 hours before your appointment at no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% cancellation fee. No-shows are charged in full.
What areas do you serve?
We serve the greater metro area We serve Austin, TX and surrounding areas — Round Rockmdash; Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Georgetown, Kyle, Buda, Lakeway, Bee Cave, Manor, Leander, and Hutto. Not sure? Just ask us.
What if something gets damaged?
We treat your home and belongings with care. In the unlikely event of damage, we are fully insured and will make it right.
Can you assemble any brand?
Yes — IKEA, Wayfair, Amazon, Ashley, Target, Costco, Walmart and more. If it came in a box, we can build it.
Are your Easers background checked?
Yes — every pro is vetted before their first job. We only work with people we’d trust in our own home. And you don’t pay until you say the job is done.
