About AssembleAtEase

Austin-based. Locally owned. Built on doing good work and treating people right.

We started because assembly should be easy

AssembleAtEase was founded right here in Austin, Texas, out of a simple frustration — putting together furniture and hanging things on walls takes way more time and effort than it should, especially when you're moving, furnishing a new home, or just trying to get your life in order.

We built this service so Austin homeowners, renters, and business owners can skip the frustration and get it done right the first time. Whether it's a single IKEA bookshelf or a full home office setup, we show up on time, do the job properly, and leave your space clean.

Every technician is background-checked, experienced, and treats your home like their own. We're licensed and insured, and we never ask for payment until you're fully satisfied with the work.
