Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we handle your information.

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information AssembleAtEase LLC collects, how we use and share it, and the choices and rights you have. It applies to our website, booking platform, and customer and assembler portals. By using the platform, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Introduction

AssembleAtEase LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates www.assembleatease.com and provides furniture assembly and home setup marketplace services based on current coverage in your area. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, create an account, or book services through our platform.

2. Information We Collect

From Customers:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and service address provided when booking
  • Service details, scheduling preferences, and booking history
  • Payment method details — collected and tokenized directly by Stripe, Inc. We never see or store your full card number; Stripe stores your card securely and we retain only a Stripe customer ID and last-four digits for rebilling purposes
  • Communications you send us via email or our contact form

From Assemblers (Service Professionals):

  • Full legal name, email, phone, city, and state
  • Government-issued ID (driver's license or passport) submitted during identity verification
  • Selfie photograph submitted during identity verification
  • Background check consent collected as a condition of the Independent Contractor Agreement (checks may be conducted at the Company's discretion; not all assemblers are background-checked at this time)
  • Service categories, experience, and profile information you provide
  • Bank account, payout, or payment information submitted to our payment processor or otherwise provided for lawful earnings disbursement

Automatically Collected:

  • IP address, browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time on site, click paths, and referring URLs
  • Cookies and local storage (see Section 6 below)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process bookings, manage payments, and deliver purchased services
  • To verify assembler identity before platform approval
  • To communicate with you about bookings, receipts, schedule changes, and support
  • To send transactional notifications (confirmations, status updates, completion alerts)
  • To send promotional communications — you may opt out at any time
  • To improve our platform, diagnose technical issues, and analyze usage trends
  • To comply with legal obligations, prevent fraud, and enforce our Terms of Service

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as described below.

Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party companies to perform functions on our behalf. These providers may access your information only to perform services for us, are contractually required to safeguard it, and may not use it for their own purposes. The categories of providers we work with include:

  • Payment processing — a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor (Stripe, Inc.) securely handles payments, card-on-file storage, and identity verification. We never see or store your full card number. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Cloud hosting & infrastructure — secure, U.S.-based cloud providers host our database, accounts, and platform data.
  • Communications & email delivery — email service providers deliver booking confirmations, receipts, and status updates.
  • Customer relationship management — a CRM provider helps us manage inquiries, follow-ups, and waitlist sign-ups.
  • Analytics — analytics providers (such as Google Analytics) help us understand how visitors use our site. Analytics are loaded only after you consent to cookies (see Section 6).

Assigned Assemblers

Before an offer is accepted, assemblers receive only the limited job information needed to decide whether to accept it. After an assigned assembler accepts an active booking, we may share your name, service address, appointment and job details, phone number, and email address when needed for access, arrival, delay, or safety coordination. Assemblers are contractually limited to using that information for the active booking and must not retain or use it for off-platform solicitation. Terminal bookings should no longer expose contact details through the Easer portal. We do not sell your personal information to any third party for advertising purposes.

Legal & Safety Disclosures

We may disclose information when we believe in good faith it is necessary to comply with the law, a subpoena, or a government request; to enforce our Terms; to detect, prevent, or address fraud or security issues; or to protect the rights, property, or safety of AssembleAtEase, our users, or the public.

Business Transfers

If AssembleAtEase is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any change in ownership or use of your personal information, and of any choices you may have.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption in transit, row-level security at our database layer, a PCI-compliant card vault for payment data, and token-based authentication for portal access. However, no system is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and browser local storage for:

  • Essential cookies: Session tokens and authentication state (always active)
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics 4 — loaded only after you click "Accept" on our cookie banner. If you decline, GA4 is not loaded and no analytics data is collected for your session.

You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or adjusting settings in your browser. Where supported, we honor browser-based opt-out signals such as Global Privacy Control (GPC).

7. Data Retention

We retain customer booking and financial records for 3 years unless a longer period is required for tax, dispute, fraud, insurance, or other legal purposes. Government ID images and identity selfies are collected and retained by Stripe under Stripe's identity-data retention controls; AssembleAtEase stores verification status and Stripe session identifiers, not copies of the raw identity documents. If we conduct background checks, related consent records are retained as required by the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA). Deletion requests are reviewed against these retention and legal-hold requirements.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements. To submit a deletion request, email service@assembleatease.com with subject line "Data Deletion Request." We will respond within 30 days.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a machine-readable export of your data by emailing service@assembleatease.com with subject line "Data Export Request."
  • Right to Opt Out of Marketing: Click "Unsubscribe" in any marketing email, or email us at any time

9. California Residents — CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) / CPRA:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
  • The right to delete your personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising. To exercise your CCPA rights, email service@assembleatease.com with subject line "CCPA Privacy Request."

10. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not directed to individuals under age 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 18, contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our platform constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
