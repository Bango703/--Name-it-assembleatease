How it works

Three steps to your first job

No complicated onboarding. Apply, get verified, and start receiving jobs right in Austin.

01
Apply & get verified

Fill out a short application online. We verify your identity and run a standard background check. Most applicants hear back within 24 hours.

02
Get jobs sent to you

When a job matches your location and availability, we send you an offer. You see the job details and price before you accept. No pressure — take what fits.

03
Complete & get paid

Do the job, mark it done in your dashboard, and payment is processed. We handle all customer billing — you just focus on the work.

The work

What you’ll be doing

We focus on six service types — all assembly and installation work in the Austin area. No plumbing, no electrical, no heavy construction.

Furniture Assembly
IKEA, Wayfair, Amazon, and flat-pack from any retailer. Beds, wardrobes, desks, sofas, shelving.
TV Mounting
Wall mount installation for any TV size, any wall type. Cable management included.
Smart Home Setup
Nest thermostats, Ring cameras, smart locks, and other connected home devices.
Fitness Equipment
Treadmills, exercise bikes, home gyms, pull-up racks, and weight benches.
Outdoor & Playsets
Swing sets, trampolines, patio furniture, outdoor storage, and pergola assembly.
Office Assembly
Standing desks, office chairs, shelving units, and workstation setups.
