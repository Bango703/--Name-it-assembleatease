Common questions

FAQ

Do you bring your own tools?
Yes — we arrive fully equipped with everything needed to complete the job. You don't need to provide any tools or equipment.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Austin, TX and surrounding areas — including Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Georgetown, Kyle, Buda, Lakeway, Bee Cave, Manor, Leander, and Hutto. Not sure if we cover your zip? Just ask us.
How quickly can you come?
We offer same-day and next-day availability for most services. Once you submit a request we'll confirm your appointment within 1 hour.
What's your cancellation policy?
We require at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule. Cancellations made within 24 hours of your scheduled appointment are subject to a cancellation fee. This protects our professionals who reserved that time slot for you.
When do I pay?
Payment is processed securely online after the job is completed to your satisfaction. We accept all major credit and debit cards. We do not accept cash, Zelle, or Venmo — all payments go through our secure platform.
What if something gets damaged?
We treat your home and belongings with care. In the unlikely event of damage, we are fully insured and will make it right.
Do I need to be home?
Yes, we ask that someone 18+ be present for the duration of the job. This ensures you can approve placement, mounting height, and final results.
Can you assemble any brand?
Yes — IKEA, Wayfair, Amazon, Ashley, Target, Costco, Walmart and more. If it came in a box, we can build it.
What's your minimum charge?
Our minimum service fee is $69. This covers most small jobs — nightstands, stools, small shelves, and similar single items.
Are your technicians background checked?
Yes — every technician is licensed, insured, and background checked before joining our team.
Do you offer warranties on your work?
We do not offer a general satisfaction warranty. All our pros are fully insured — if a professional damages your property or belongings during a job, it will be covered. Report any damage within 48 hours of job completion.
