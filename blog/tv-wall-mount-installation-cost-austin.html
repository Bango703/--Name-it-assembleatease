TV Wall Mount Installation Cost in Austin, TX: What You'll Actually Pay

May 1, 2026 • 3 min read

Whether you're upgrading your living room, creating a home theater, or mounting a TV in your Austin bedroom, wall mounting is a smart choice. It saves space, improves viewing angles, and looks sleek. But what does it actually cost to have a professional install your TV mount in Austin?

The short answer: installation typically ranges from $150 to $500, depending on your wall type, mount complexity, and whether you need additional services. Let's break down what influences the price and help you plan your budget.

Factors That Affect TV Wall Mount Installation Costs

Installation costs in Austin aren't one-size-fits-all. Several variables determine what you'll pay for professional mounting:

  • Wall Type: Drywall is straightforward and affordable. Brick, concrete, or stone walls (common in older Austin homes) require specialized drilling and cost more.
  • Mount Type: Fixed mounts are cheaper to install. Full-motion (articulating) arms require more labor and precision.
  • TV Size and Weight: Larger, heavier TVs need stronger reinforcement and careful installation.
  • Cable Management: Running cables behind walls, hiding cords, or installing power outlets adds time and cost.
  • Stud Placement: If studs aren't conveniently located, installation becomes more complex.
  • Existing Damage: Repairs to walls before mounting increase the overall price.

Should You DIY or Hire a Professional in Austin?

It's tempting to save money and mount your TV yourself. But here's why professional installation often makes sense:

  • Safety: A TV falling off a wall is dangerous and expensive. Professionals ensure proper anchoring for TVs of all sizes.
  • Warranty Protection: Many TV manufacturers void warranties if you use non-professional installation.
  • Wall Expertise: Austin's mix of older homes with plaster, brick, and newer drywall requires experienced judgment.
  • Cable Management: Professionals know how to route cables neatly—essential in Austin's modern homes and apartments.
  • Time Savings: What might take you an afternoon takes a pro 45 minutes.

If you do decide to DIY, invest in a quality stud finder, level, and appropriate anchors for your wall type. But for peace of mind, AssembleAtEase offers professional TV mounting across Austin at competitive rates with guaranteed safety and accuracy.

Hidden Costs to Budget For

Beyond the basic installation, Austin homeowners sometimes encounter additional expenses:

  1. Mount Purchase: Mounts range from $30 to $300+ depending on quality and features.
  2. Cable Supplies: HDMI cables, power conduit, and clips ($30–$80).
  3. Outlet Installation: If your TV location lacks nearby power, electrician fees apply ($150–$300).
  4. Wall Studs Reinforcement: Older Austin homes sometimes need additional reinforcement ($75–$150).
  5. Drywall Patching: After hiding cables behind walls, professionals patch and paint ($50–$150).

Austin-Specific Considerations

Austin's diverse home architecture affects installation costs. Historic homes in neighborhoods like Mueller or South Congress often have plaster walls, which require more careful drilling. Modern apartments and newer builds in North Austin typically have standard drywall, making installation straightforward and affordable.

Additionally, Austin's hot climate means considering heat dissipation around your TV mount. Professionals ensure proper ventilation to prevent overheating—especially important for mounted TVs in direct sunlight.
