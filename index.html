Common questions

FAQ

How much does it cost
Standalone visits start at $99 with flat-rate pricing before you book. Same-visit add-ons can be lower, starting at $15, when bundled with a main service. See our full pricing page for exact item pricing.
When do I pay
Your card is secured at booking. Standard card holds are captured after completion; larger jobs may collect a 25% deposit at booking with the balance charged after completion. We accept all major credit and debit cards through Stripe.
How quickly can you come
We offer same-day and next-day availability for most services. Once you submit a booking we confirm your appointment within 1 hour.
What’s your cancellation policy
Cancel at least 24 hours before your appointment at no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% cancellation fee. No-shows are charged in full.
What areas do you serve
We serve Austin, TX and nearby areas including Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Georgetown, Kyle, Buda, Lakeway, Bee Cave, Manor, Leander, and Hutto. Not sure Just ask us.
What if something gets damaged
We treat your home and belongings with care. In the unlikely event of damage, we are fully insured and will make it right.
Can you assemble any brand
Yes — IKEA, Wayfair, Amazon, Ashley, Target, Costco, Walmart and more. If it came in a box, we can build it.
Are your Easers background checked
Yes — every pro is vetted before their first job. We only work with people we’d trust in our own home.
