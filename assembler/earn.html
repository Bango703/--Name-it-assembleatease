Common questions
Assembler FAQ
Do I need my own tools? ⌄
You'll need basic hand tools (screwdrivers, Allen keys, drill). We don't supply tools, but most assembly jobs only require a basic toolkit. When you apply, you'll indicate what tools you own.
How does payment work? ⌄
The platform takes a 20% service fee from each completed job. You keep 80%. The owner records your payout directly — you’ll receive it promptly after job completion. We’re constantly improving the payout speed.
Can I decline a job? ⌄
Absolutely. When the owner assigns you a job, you'll receive an email with accept/decline links. If you decline, the booking is returned to the owner for reassignment. We ask that you decline promptly so the customer isn’t kept waiting.
Do I need experience? ⌄
Prior assembly or handyman experience is strongly preferred, but not always required for Starter tier. We look for reliability, good communication, and a customer-first attitude as much as technical skill.
How long does approval take? ⌄
Most applications are reviewed within 48 hours. ID verification via Stripe Identity takes just a few minutes on your end. Once approved, you’ll receive a welcome email with your dashboard login link.
What if a customer is unhappy? ⌄
All disputes are handled through our owner. We have a dispute resolution process that protects both assemblers and customers. As long as you do quality work and communicate clearly, disputes are rare.
Is this a W-2 job or 1099? ⌄
Assemblers are independent contractors (1099). You’re your own boss — you set your availability, accept the jobs you want, and are responsible for your own taxes and insurance.
