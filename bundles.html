Why a bundle?

A bundle isn’t a discount gimmick — it’s one trip that handles a whole room. One visit, one reviewed pro, one upfront price, everything set up and cleaned up together. You skip the back-and-forth of booking pieces separately and the room is simply done.

Common questions

Can I change what’s in a bundle?

Yes. A bundle just pre-fills the booking with the usual items for that room. You can add or remove anything before you confirm, and the total updates live.

How is a bundle priced?

Each item is priced at its normal flat rate and added up — no hidden bundle markup. You see the full total (plus a flat service-call fee and tax) before you book.

When am I charged?

Never up front. Your card is held when you book and only charged after the job is completed.

Do I earn rewards on a bundle?

Yes — you earn 5% back in AssembleCash after the job is completed, to use toward a future booking.
