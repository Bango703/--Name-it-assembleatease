About AssembleAtEase

Built for homes that need things done right.

AssembleAtEase handles furniture assembly, mounting, and home setup with clear pricing, careful pros, and payment only after the work is complete.

Clear online booking Identity-verified pros Fast follow-up
Why we started

Assembly should feel easy, not like another chore.

AssembleAtEase was founded by Travis Gibson after seeing how much time people lose to moving, mounting, assembling, and setting up a home.

We built the service for homeowners, renters, and business owners who want the job handled cleanly the first time. Whether it is a single IKEA bookshelf, a mounted TV, a home office, or a whole-room setup, we show up prepared and leave the space ready to use.

Every pro is reviewed before dispatch, expected to show relevant experience, and expected to treat your home with care. We keep the booking clear, the work careful, and the follow-up responsive.
