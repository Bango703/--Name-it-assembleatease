Pricing FAQ

Common questions about pricing

When am I charged ⌄
At checkout, Stripe securely verifies your card and shows the full total before you confirm. You may see a temporary “pending” hold on your account; the actual charge is processed after the job is complete. Cancel within the policy window and the hold is released. We never see or store your card number.
Are parts/materials included ⌄
Labor is included in all prices above. Parts or materials (e.g., drywall compound, faucets, smart devices, wall anchors) are not included unless specifically listed. We’ll tell you exactly what to have ready before we arrive, or we can pick up materials for a small fee.
What if my job takes longer than expected ⌄
We quote flat rates for most standard jobs. If a job turns out to be significantly more complex (e.g., damaged hardware, hidden structural issues), we’ll communicate with you before proceeding. You always have the option to approve or decline additional charges.
Is there a service-call or travel fee ⌄
Yes — a service-call fee covers your pro’s travel and appointment setup. It’s $35 in Austin core ZIPs, $50 in near-suburb ZIPs, and $65 in far-suburb ZIPs. It’s always shown as its own line before you confirm — never hidden — so there are no surprises at checkout.
What’s your cancellation policy ⌄
Plans change — no problem. Cancel or reschedule free up to 24 hours before your appointment. Inside 24 hours, a late-cancel fee may apply because a pro has already reserved that time for you. If something unusual comes up, contact us and we’ll work with you.
Can I get a custom quote ⌄
Absolutely. For large projects, whole-home setups, commercial spaces, or anything not listed above, contact us and we’ll respond with a free custom quote within a few hours.
