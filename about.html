Why we started

Assembly should feel easy, not like another chore.

AssembleAtEase was founded right here in Austin by Travis Gibson after seeing how much time people lose to moving, mounting, assembling, and setting up a home.

We built the service for homeowners, renters, and business owners who want the job handled cleanly the first time. Whether it is a single IKEA bookshelf, a mounted TV, a home office, or a whole-room setup, we show up prepared and leave the space ready to use.

Every pro is reviewed before dispatch, expected to show relevant experience, and expected to treat your home with care. No surprise fee games. No rushed work. Secure payment at booking.
