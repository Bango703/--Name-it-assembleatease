How it works

1Book and complete a job

Once your setup is done and paid, you earn 5% of the total in AssembleCash.

2Check your balance

Look it up anytime on the Track My Booking page — just verify your email.

3Apply it next time

At checkout, confirm your email with a one-time code and apply up to $20 toward the booking.

Good to know

  • AssembleCash is future-booking credit — it has no cash value and cannot be withdrawn.
  • It can only be used toward future AssembleAtEase bookings.
  • You earn it only after a booking is completed and payment is captured.
  • Up to $20 can be applied per booking.
  • AssembleCash expires 180 days after you earn it.
  • If a booking is refunded, any AssembleCash earned from it is reversed.
