Same-Day Handyman Service in Austin TX

April 24, 2026 • 4 min read • Home Tips

Austin is a fast-moving city, and homeowners here often need things done on short notice. Guests arriving tomorrow. A new sofa just delivered with no time to build it. A TV mount that has been sitting in a box for three weeks and you finally decided today is the day. Same-day handyman service exists precisely for moments like these. Here is what to know about getting a professional out the same day you call.

What Can Actually Be Done Same-Day in Austin?

The honest answer: most jobs. AssembleAtEase offers same-day availability for bookings made before 12:00 PM on weekdays, and before 10:00 AM on weekends. Here is a sample of what is routinely handled same-day:

Furniture Assembly Beds, sofas, desks, dressers, bookshelves from IKEA, Wayfair, Amazon and more
TV Mounting Any size TV on any wall type — we bring the mount and all hardware
Smart Home Thermostat, doorbell, cameras, smart locks — installation and app setup
Junk Removal Single items to small room cleanouts — same day if booked before noon
Hanging & Mounting Curtain rods, gallery walls, shelves, mirrors
Minor Repairs Door alignment, cabinet hardware, drywall patches under 6 inches

How to Get a Same-Day Appointment

Booking same-day with AssembleAtEase is straightforward:

  • Visit the booking form at assembleatease.com and describe what you need
  • Select "As soon as possible" or choose today's date and preferred time window
  • Submit your card — it is authorized (not charged) at booking
  • We confirm availability within 60 minutes and send assembler details
  • Your assembler arrives with all tools and supplies needed for the job
  • Payment is captured only after you confirm the job is done right

What Jobs Require More Lead Time?

Most jobs work same-day, but some situations benefit from scheduling 24–48 hours ahead:

  • Large furniture packages — 6+ items, full bedroom sets, or wardrobe systems that require a 4+ hour appointment block
  • Whole-home move-in setups — we may send two techs, which requires more scheduling coordination
  • Custom or commercial work — office fit-outs, commercial display installs, or projects requiring materials procurement
  • Weekend mornings during peak season — Spring (March–May) and Fall (September–October) are our busiest periods in Austin. Book a day ahead if possible during these months

Austin Service Areas for Same-Day

We service all Austin zip codes same-day, including North Austin (78753, 78758, 78727), Central Austin (78701, 78703, 78704), South Austin (78745, 78748), East Austin (78702, 78721), and West Austin (78746). We also cover Cedar Park, Round Rock, Pflugerville, Kyle, and Buda for same-day slots when available.

What Does Same-Day Service Cost?

Same-day bookings with AssembleAtEase are the same flat rate as scheduled bookings — no rush surcharge. We believe that showing up when you need us is part of the service, not an excuse to charge more.
